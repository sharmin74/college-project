How do you buy a Camel ?



1. First step: install and configure Metamask

Metamask is a Web3 wallet that interacts with Opensea and other Web3 sites and DAPs


This procedure is valid for the following browsers:
❏ Chrome
❏ Brave Browser
❏ Microsoft Edge (Chromium based version)
❏ Go to the Metamask website: https://metamask.io/ and click download on the top right corner

❏ Then click on “Install Meta Mask for Chrome”
❏ Run Metamask by clicking on the extension icon and initialize it. Check this video explaining how to do it: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Af_lQ1zUnoM
❏ You can buy ETH on regular exchanges (like Coinbase or Kraken), and send it to your Metamask address



2. Second step: connect Metamask to Opensea

❏ Go to Opensea: https://opensea.io/ and press on the account icon in the top right corner



❏ You will be prompted to Sign In with Metamask
Click on “Sign In”
❏ Choose the account to be connected to Opensea (1) In a “fresh” wallet you should have only one account
❏ Press “Next (2)
❏ Press “Connect” (3)



3. Third step: Search for Camels Opensea

❏ Search for “Arabian Camels” on the search box
❏ Press on the “Arabian Camels” that has the blue check mark (beware of fake camels)
❏ Et voila … you have Camels in front of you ready to go to your caravan



4. Fourth step: Choose your Camel

You can make some choices:
❏ In the left side you have filters depending on the status of the camels:
❏ Buy now
❏ On auction
❏ New
❏ Has Offers On the right side you have buttons that allows you to see individual items or bundles and sort the items (my preferred is Price low to High)



You can further refine your search, by selecting specific traits (scroll down on the left menu):
❏ Background
❏ Base
❏ Ears
❏ Eyes
❏ Fur … (and other traits as shown here) If you want a lovely pink Camel, you must click on “Fur”, then select “Pink fur



5. Fifth step: Buy your Camel

1- Click on a Camel of your preference, and press on “Buy Now”


2 - Confirm on the “Checkout” pop-up window



❏ (1) You can Edit the GAS FEE (the fees paid to Ethereum miners to process your transaction)
❏ It’s better to leave it on “Average” or “Fast” and click “Save”
❏ (2) Click on “Confirm” and Wait for transaction confirmation
❏ After transaction confirmation you are a proud owner of a Camel.
