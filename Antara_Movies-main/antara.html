ANTARA FILM & TRANSMEDIA FRANCHISE

The Antara IP covers the life and adventures of Antara Ibn Shaddad, a desert warrior from ancient pre Islamic Arabia. Antara was a black slave who won his freedom on the battle field. He became a dignified knight by his sagacious character, valour in battle, and magnanimous talent with poetry and words. The Franchise includes a feature film with 4 sequels, a crypto infused multi-player game, a comic, and a strong brand with a bold merchandising and licensing strategy
