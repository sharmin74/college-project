ABOUT US

The Arabian Camels have become an iconic international brand with one of the strongest and tightly knit NFT communities in the NFT space. Arabian Camels is a collection of 12,012 uniquely generated digital collectibles living on the Ethereum blockchain. Each Camel is programmatically generated from over 180 assets with 8 traits, such as expressions, accessories and headwear etc. Arabian Camels are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. Our community will be able to benefit by partaking in our productions and projects, earning, learning, voting, and contributing to the democratisation and decentralisation of the transmedia space. Due to enhanced risk mitigation offered through smart contracts, for the first time in history, movie and gaming investors will also be able to benefit from instant and transparent earnings via revenue streams on platforms supported by blockchain technology. Each camel holder has ownership and commercial usage rights over their NFT
